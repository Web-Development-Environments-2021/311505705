About Me

I was born in Poland in 1935. During the Holocaust I hid in different villages with Polish families. I lost my father and mother, and the fate of my brother is unknown to this day.

After the war, at the age of 10, I was taken to a Jewish orphanage near the town of Lodge where I met my good friend Esti who I am in contact with to this day.

From there I immigrated to Israel in 1948 by the Jewish organization "Coordination".

At first I was at the "Onin" institution near Kfar Saba and then I came to the Ben Shemen youth village.

I served in the Nahal at Yad Mordechai. In 1953 I came to Kibbutz Shoval where I met Avri, we got married, started a family and to this day I live in the kibbutz.

I have a great family! 4 children (Ronen, Tzafrir, Marganit and Nimrod) and 10 grandchildren.

Today I am a happy person, I am busy with my family and it is very good for me.

During the years at the kibbutz, I worked as the kibbutz secretary, in charge of the commune, in the nursery and sewing workshop.

I have many hobbies among them knitting, reading books, doing a lot of sports and having fun with the family.
